What is it?

In this psychological short film, Tom, a confident psychotherapist, faces an unsettling session that pushes him to confront his own fears. The trailer teases a tense exploration of the battle between the Id, Ego, and Superego, hinting at dark secrets buried deep in the subconscious. With eerie visuals and rising tension, it asks the haunting question: What happens when you're forced to face your deepest fears, face to face?
