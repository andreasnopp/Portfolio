2. Collection and Storage of Personal Data, as well as Type and Purpose of Use Contact Form

3. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of data submitted via the contact form is carried out based on Article 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). By submitting the form, you consent to the processing of your provided data for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. You can revoke your consent at any time by contacting me (Andreas Nopp).

4. Data Retention

Your message and the associated data will only be stored as long as necessary to process your inquiry and will be deleted afterward. Since this website is not operated for commercial purposes, there are no legal retention obligations.

5. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- Right to access – Request a copy of the personal data stored.
- Right to rectification – Request correction of incorrect or incomplete data.
- Right to erasure – Request deletion of your data unless legal obligations require its retention.
- Right to restriction of processing – Request limited processing of your data.
- Right to data portability – Receive your data in a structured format.
- Right to object – Object to data processing under certain conditions.

- If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact Andreas Nopp via the contact form, or via E-Mail at contact@andreasnopp.com .

If you believe that your data has been processed unlawfully, you have the right to file a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.
Embedded YouTube videos are loaded using YouTube’s enhanced privacy mode (youtube-nocookie.com). This ensures that no cookies are set by YouTube until you actively play a video. However, once a video is played, YouTube may process user data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as needed. The current version will always be available on this website.
