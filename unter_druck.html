My Roles in This Project: DoP, Editing

Director: Dominic Hochrainer

________________________________________

What is it?

The first short film I ever worked on—together with many friends and colleagues.
This short film was created during my time at Medienkolleg Innsbruck and portrays the psychological profile of a young man driven to madness by his boss.

What went well?

The project was a lot of fun.
Since this was our very first short film shoot, everything was completely new to us.
We had better equipment and actors than in any previous project.
Through this experience, I gained a clearer understanding of film production workflows.

What could be improved?

Some disagreements within the team (e.g., task distribution).
Better time management, as film shoots always take several hours longer than planned.
Learning how to work with actors.
